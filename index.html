05 — Philosophy

Research is not a destination—
it is a disciplined practice.

I believe the most meaningful AI work begins with questions, not answers. Each experiment is a conversation with data—requiring patience, rigor, and the willingness to be surprised.

Model evaluation is where understanding deepens. I don't chase metrics blindly—I study what they reveal about the system's behavior, its failures, and its boundaries. This discipline of careful observation is what separates engineering from craft.

The bridge from research to production is where intention meets reality. I strive to build systems that are not only accurate but robust, interpretable, and genuinely useful—translating laboratory insights into solutions that serve people.
